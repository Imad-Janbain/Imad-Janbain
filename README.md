## 🔬 About

I build deep learning systems that turn messy environmental and geoscientific data into reliable forecasts. My research sits at the intersection of **hydrology, climate science, and modern ML** — with a strong focus on time series, physics-informed models, and explainability.

Currently designing production-oriented forecasting pipelines that combine classical hydrological signal processing (wavelets, decomposition) with deep architectures (GRU, LSTM, Transformer, KAN) and LLM-based natural-language reporting.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 💧 Groundwater Level Forecasting — Wavelet + GRU/LSTM
Wavelet-decomposed deep learning pipeline (MODWT, LA(8), 4 levels) combined with ERA5 climate reanalysis. Optuna hyperparameter search, full visualization module, evaluated with KGE / NSE / CFD metrics.

### 🌊 River Discharge Forecasting with LLM Explainability
Production-ready repository forecasting USGS streamflow with ARIMA / XGBoost / BiLSTM and Claude-powered natural-language reporting. End-to-end: ingestion → modeling → evaluation → narrative report.

### 🛰️ Sentinel-1 Flood Segmentation
Deep learning semantic segmentation of flooded areas from Sentinel-1 SAR imagery. Preprocessing pipeline for radar data and U-Net-style architectures.

### 🧠 KAN-Based Hydroclimatic Forecasting
Research study introducing **Kolmogorov–Arnold Networks** into a wavelet-based hydroclimatic pipeline. Evaluates KAN in four roles against a Wavelet–GRU baseline.

### 📄 OCR + LLM Card Information Extraction
Two-stage pipeline: OCR extraction followed by LLM-based semantic understanding and structuring of card documents.
